FAQs

Can I change programme after registration?

Yes. You can change programme on the first semester of year 1.

What is the requirement to register industry training?

Below are the overall requirements:-

  1. No subject that scored grade E or F, except retake and gets grade E and Upper.
  2. Taken 109 credit hours or more.
  3. CGPA >= 2.0

What is the minimum requirements to study a FCI programme?

CGPA >= 2.75

How do I enrol as a research student?

Once your application has been accepted, enrolment is a two-step process: -

  1. Administrative Enrolment: completed by the student
  2. Course and Subject Enrolment: completed by SMP.

You can complete your Administrative Enrolment once you accept the enrolment Terms and Conditions.
